About Us

Empowering the next generation of healthcare professionals through excellence in education, innovation, and leadership.

About Apollo Knowledge

Apollo Knowledge is a pioneering initiative by the Apollo Hospitals Group, dedicated to transforming healthcare education in India and beyond. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic excellence and real-world healthcare needs, equipping students with the skills, knowledge, and values required to excel in allied health sciences. Backed by decades of healthcare leadership, Apollo Knowledge integrates advanced learning methodologies, global best practices, and strong industry linkages to nurture the healthcare leaders of tomorrow.

About AHERF

The Apollo Hospitals Educational and Research Foundation (AHERF) is committed to revolutionizing healthcare by leading clinical and translational research that paves the way for personalized and precision medicine. The foundation's research endeavors are centered on discovering and developing innovative biomarkers, assay systems, cell therapies, and diagnostics to tackle critical health challenges such as cancer, diabetes, autoimmune disorders, and other widespread pathological conditions. At the cutting edge of medical science, AHERF is pioneering the development of novel therapeutic and diagnostic strategies, harnessing the potential of circulatory biomarkers and cell therapies and furthering the progress of personalized and precision medicine.

Our Mission

  • Undertake and promote translational and clinical research in major medical disciplines, focusing on translating research into practical applications.
  • Develop a competent workforce through training and skill development programs.
  • Provide clinical infrastructure and expertise for developing, establishing, and validating medical devices and assays.

Our Vision

To be a world-class clinical and translational research entity encompassing personalized and precision medicine for the hospital and biotechnology community.

Our Strengths

  • Exclusive not-for-profit, non-governmental research foundation for the largest healthcare chain Apollo Hospitals Group
  • DSIR recognition for over 14 years
  • Supported by NABH-certified Institutional Ethics Committees
  • High-quality scientific/technical expertise in research designs
  • Centralized and shared resources to carry out research
  • In-depth research expertise knowledge in molecular biology

Our Leadership

Dr. Prathap C Reddy

Founder & Chairman, Apollo Hospitals Group

Dr. Prathap C Reddy, the founder-chairman of Apollo Hospitals, is the architect of modern Indian healthcare. He is a compassionate humanitarian dedicated to making world-class healthcare accessible and affordable for millions. He founded Apollo Hospitals in 1983, revolutionizing the private healthcare industry in India by offering international quality care at a fraction of western costs. This was Apollo's first act of social responsibility and highlighted the company's commitment to social conscience.

Dr. Preetha Reddy

Executive Vice Chairperson, Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Limited

Dr. Preetha has championed deep clinician engagement and modern medical protocols to continuously enhance clinical outcomes. Her leadership has brought the latest treatments and practices to India, with Apollo’s clinical excellence recognized internationally, often surpassing global standards of clinical outcomes. She led the establishment of pioneering technologies, such as South Asia’s first Proton Cancer Therapy and the Zap-X, making advanced therapies more accessible and reducing the lag in adopting new treatments. Apollo Hospitals’ success stems from its 12,000+ strong clinical team and the platform it provides for delivering world-class care. Dr. Preetha’s strength lies in building this ecosystem and benchmarking clinical outcomes against global standards.

Mr V. Sivaramakrishnan

Chief Executive Officer, Apollo Knowledge

Sivaramakrishnan is renowned for turning businesses around with new initiatives bringing on board 30+ years of global experience in various sectors such as FMCG, Automobiles, e-Commerce and Education across UK, Dubai, Malaysia and India. At Apollo Knowledge, he is focusing on brand building, marketing and alliance management, key accounts management, team management, and strategic planning for new and existing markets. He will also be looking to enhance market penetration, re-engineering plan executions and operational efficiency with P&I management. Prior to his current assignment, he worked with the likes of Ford Motor Company, Ford India, P&G India, HCL, Oxford University Press India and other prestigious institutions.

Dr. H Vinod Bhat

Vice Chancellor, The Apollo University

Prof. H. Vinod Bhat took charge as the Vice Chancellor of The Apollo University on 1.7.2022. He has been a medical doctor, teacher, researcher and administrator for the past 34 years. As the Vice Chancellor of the Manipal Academy of Higher Education, he was responsible for the University being conferred the coveted tag of Institution of Eminence, which was one of only three such conferments in the Private sector. He has been an institution builder par excellence, setting up two campuses, five University departments and over 100 University centres. He has very strong international University networks, having been the Co-Chair for the FICCI Higher Education Committee in the year 2017 – 18.
